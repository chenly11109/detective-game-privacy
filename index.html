Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Everyone's a Detective is an interactive mystery game. This policy explains how the app handles information when you use the game.

Information We Process

The app may process gameplay choices, selected cases, investigation progress, and questions you type or speak to in-game characters. Voice input is converted to text before it is used by the game.

How Information Is Used

Gameplay information is used only to run the mystery experience, generate in-game responses, maintain game state, and improve reliability. We do not sell personal information.

Network and AI Services

The app connects to our backend service to run game sessions. Some typed or spoken questions may be sent to server-side services so the game can generate relevant responses.

Microphone and Speech Recognition

If you use voice input, the app requests microphone and speech recognition permissions. Speech recognition is used to convert your speech into text for gameplay. You can disable microphone access in iOS Settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information may be processed by service providers that help operate the app, such as hosting, backend infrastructure, and speech or language processing services.

Children

The app is not directed to children as a children's app. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Contact

隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026 年 5 月 22 日

《Everyone's a Detective》是一款互动推理游戏。本政策说明 App 在你使用游戏时如何处理信息。

我们处理的信息

App 可能会处理你的游戏选择、案件选择、调查进度，以及你向游戏角色输入或通过语音说出的提问。语音输入会先转换为文字，再用于游戏体验。

信息用途

游戏信息仅用于运行推理体验、生成游戏内回复、维护游戏状态以及提升稳定性。我们不会出售个人信息。

网络与 AI 服务

App 会连接我们的后端服务来运行游戏会话。部分文字或语音转换后的问题可能会发送到服务端服务，以便游戏生成相关回复。

麦克风与语音识别

如果你使用语音输入，App 会请求麦克风和语音识别权限。语音识别用于将你的语音转换为游戏内可使用的文字。你可以在 iOS 系统设置中关闭麦克风权限。

数据共享

我们不会出售你的数据。信息可能会由帮助 App 运行的服务提供商处理，例如托管、后端基础设施、语音识别或语言处理服务。

儿童

本 App 并非专门面向儿童的儿童类 App。如果你认为儿童向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们，我们会采取适当措施。
